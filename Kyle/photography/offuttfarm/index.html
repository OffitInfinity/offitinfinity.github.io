About This Album

Bought by my great grandfather after returning from the Pacific in the late 1940's, this farm has been quite documented over her 80+ years of service and disservice. Slides record the construction of the farm house, continuing into the years before the farm would transistion into a nursery. My own photos begin the year my great grandmother passed away, continuing towards my father's passing.
Most were taken while staying on the family farm while my father was hospitalized for a month prior to his passing.
