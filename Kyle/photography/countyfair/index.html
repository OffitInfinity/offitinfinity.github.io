About This Album

The Lorain County Fair is host on the last full week of August at the fairgrounds in Wellington. For my family, have spent 3 generation in Junior Fair, attendance is tradition, including camping. 2024 was my youngest sister's final year in 4-H and likely our last year camping for a while.
I had recently been placed in a mood to take photos that year due to two reasons. First, the fair board was begun construction on their new year-round facility with plans to teardown and replace the beef barns and show barn. Second was purusing my great grandfather's film slides.
